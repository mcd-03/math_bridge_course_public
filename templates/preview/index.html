How Our Courses Work


Math Bridge Course is built with the philosophy that math should make sense. And as practing educators, we know that some topics matter a lot more than others. We have broken each of our courses into units and each of those units into the concepts that matter most. Each unit includes video instruction, examples, practice problems, and answer keys that students can use to check their progress. At the end of each unit, students are challenged with a set of mixed practice problems that cover every topic in the unit. We have taken great care to align these to the depth of knowledge and rigor required by the North Carolina End of Grade or End of Course assessments.


We've designed our courses to be completed asynchronously - you have everything you need and can access it anytime you would like. Most students complete our courses in ten to twenty hours - depending on the course and the familariity the child has with the material.
